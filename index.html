About Us

Ashte Logistics Pvt. Ltd., headquartered in Mumbai, has established a cutting-edge Container Freight Station (CFS) in the picturesque Ashte Village, Panvel, nestled near the bustling Jawaharlal Nehru Port in Navi Mumbai. The Ashte CFS sprawls over an expansive 40,000 square meters, boasting meticulously designed warehouses dedicated to facilitating seamless import and export operations. Additionally, its well-paved yard stands ready to graciously host over 2,000 Twenty-foot Equivalent Units (TEUs) concurrently, ensuring efficient cargo handling in a splendid setting.
