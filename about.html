About Us

"Ashte Logistics, headquartered in the bustling city of Mumbai, stands as a beacon of excellence in the world of logistics. With visionary leadership and a relentless commitment to innovation, Ashte Logistics has established state-of-the-art Container Freight Stations (CFS) nationwide, including a remarkable CFS in Ashte Village, Panvel, near the renowned Jawaharlal Nehru Port. This strategically positioned CFS boasts expansive warehouses for seamless import and export operations, and a vast paved yard accommodating over 3,500 TEUs, all wrapped in the charm of an area spanning 70,000 sq.mts. Backed by decades of experience in the industry, Ashte Logistics is more than just a logistics provider; it's a trusted partner. With a focus on delivering cost-effective solutions, from customs clearance to container management, Ashte Logistics ensures that your cargo flows effortlessly. With an annual capacity of 1,00,000 TEUs and a dedicated team of over 100 skilled professionals, they go the extra mile. Moreover, their commitment to security, powered by round-the-clock vigilance and robust infrastructure, is unmatched. Cutting-edge equipment, including reach-stackers and fire hydrant systems, is par for the course. Online tracking, tariff access, and streamlined processes are their hallmarks. Ashte Logistics redefines logistics, delivering a seamless experience and unwavering customer satisfaction."
