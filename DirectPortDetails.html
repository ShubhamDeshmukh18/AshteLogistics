Efficient Direct Port Delivery Services

"If you encounter challenges in clearing your cargo directly from the wharf within the specified timeframe for Direct Port Delivery, rely on us to swiftly and efficiently transfer it to a secure yard, warehouse, or alternate storage facility. State-of-the-Art Port Delivery Services

The government's Direct Port Delivery model streamlines cargo clearance for accredited importers. However, if you face time constraints, our Container Freight Station (CFS) facilities provide a seamless alternative. Equipped with bonded and non-bonded warehousing, advanced infrastructure, and digital operations, we ensure secure and efficient cargo handling.

  • We offer bonded and non-bonded warehousing.
  • We have state-of-the-art infrastructure.
  • Our operations are digitally-enabled.
  • We provide pilferage-free facilities with constant CCTV surveillance.
  • We ensure safe and hassle-free cargo transport operations.

Our CFS facilities offer a range of storage options, including bonded and non-bonded warehousing. With state-of-the-art infrastructure and round-the-clock CCTV surveillance, your cargo remains secure. Our digitally-enabled operations minimize delays, providing a hassle-free experience for your cargo transport needs.
